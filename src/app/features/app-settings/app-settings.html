How Geo-fencing Works

Employees must be within the specified Radius of any authorized location to punch in.

If no locations are added, geo-fencing is disabled and employees can punch in from any location.

How to get Lat/Long:
  1. Open Google Maps on your computer.
  2. Right-click the place or area on the map.
  3. This will open a pop-up window. You can find your latitude and longitude at the top.
  4. Left-click on the coordinates to automatically copy them to your clipboard.
